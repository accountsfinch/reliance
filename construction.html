Who Are We.

Reliance Construction is a wholly Zimbabwean owned construction company.Established in 1992 as a building and engineering construction company its is registered with the ministry of Local government , National housing and Public works. Reliance is a member of the Zimbabwe Building Contractors Association (ZBCA) and National. Employment Council for the construction industry.Relinace construction is a sub-division of Reliance Holdings P.v.t Ltd. Reliance Construction offers specialized expertise Infrastructural Development, Commercial and Residential Houses as well as Civil engineering and Project Management.
