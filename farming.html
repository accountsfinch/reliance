Few Words About Us

Wondrous Works Investments Pvt Limited have the equipment knowledge and experience required to undertake farming projects which normally involves planting and harvesting. We have the capacity to plant fresh produce in our farm and also the harvesting part is carried out by our three harvesters that are at the farm. We also have a huge warehouse and distribution center in the high density suburb to capture the low end of the market. In the hardware distribution side of our business, we have the distribution points across all the major shopping centers in three major cities namely Bulawayo, Harare and Gweru.
